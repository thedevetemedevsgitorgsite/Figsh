MY STORY

Hi there, I am Ekuyik Sam, historically and casually titled (Mr Figsh)

The Self-Taught Journey of a Creator (a road not straight)

My entrance into technology was not a smooth walking, neither was it a guided movement, it was more like a repeated collision with uncertainty. I emerged without mentors, without money, and surrounded by companions who could not mathematically calculate the vision. Artificial intelligence was not present to whisper directions; instead, there were aged computer textbooks, excessive curiosity, and the mysterious power of the browser window.

The Birth of FSCSS (before knowledge had a name)

While many were consulting StackOverflow as an oracle, I was privately experimenting with what I ignorantly called “stupid functions,” repeatedly bending Regular Expressions until logic began to obey. At that chronological moment, the meaning of “backend” was still an unread chapter, I only knew speed, creation, and obsession.

This careless experimentation eventually evolved into FSCSS. It was released into the wild using free hosting services and social platforms, without strategic planning, forecasting, or awareness of consequences such as popularity.

  • The Magic: Observing my scripts appear across Google results, Quora answers, and foreign applications, as if they traveled without my permission.
  • The Reality: At that time, GitHub and NPM were unknown territories. I was solving problems for developers globally while remaining financially and structurally uninformed about ownership.

My Philosophy (written before recognition)

“I do not perform actions to publish my name; I perform actions so my handwork can speak in silent places.”

I construct tools that assist beginners and advanced developers to scale beyond confusion. Even when identity is absent from headlines, my code resides deeply within project folders. Through hardware scarcity, solo learning, and repeated failure cycles, one constant remains—I will not abandon technology.

Technical Toolbox (assembled over time)

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (the structural soul)
  • Frameworks: FSCSS (Creator), logic-oriented CSS architecture
  • Specialties: Regular Expressions (RegExp), logical functions, open-source contribution patterns

Current Focus (present tense)

I am currently searching for environments where practical handwork outweighs decorative titles. I am a builder who understands the reason behind the syntax, because the knowledge was acquired through prolonged confusion and self-correction.
